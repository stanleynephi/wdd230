Site Name

Site name for the chamber of commerce website is 'Accra Traders Chamber'

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website it to provide a place on the internet where various traders can advertise their products or services and connect with various clients from different places looking for the services needed.

Site Scenarios

  1. What are the benefits of becoming a member of the chamber, and how can I sign up for membership?
  2. How can I list my bussiness in the chamber's directory, and what promotional opportunities are available for membership?
  3. What resources and guidance does the chamber provide for individuals looking to start a new bussiness in the area?

Color Schema

Carefully selected colors forthe chamber of commerce website are as follows:

  1. Blue - For background color of the website
  2. White - For the textual content on the website
  3. Black - For heading and other textual content

Typography

The following fonts have been selected to be used on the website:

  1. Roboto - Serif font, suitable for headings (H1)
  2. Montserrat - Sans-serif font, suitable for paragraphs.
